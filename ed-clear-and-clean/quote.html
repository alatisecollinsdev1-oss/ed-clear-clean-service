FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question not listed here? Call or WhatsApp us and we'll be happy to help.

How quickly can you come out?
+
We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours. For urgent jobs, we offer same-day availability depending on our schedule. The best way to get a fast response is to call or WhatsApp us directly on +44 7368 467662 .
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
+
No — we bring all our own professional-grade cleaning equipment and products. You don't need to provide anything. We use eco-friendly products where possible and always choose products that are safe for your home, children, and pets.
What areas do you cover?
+
We are based in Huddersfield and serve Huddersfield, Leeds, and the surrounding areas of West Yorkshire. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just give us a call or send us a WhatsApp and we'll let you know.
How do I pay?
+
We handle payment directly — there is no online payment on this website. Once we've discussed your job and agreed on a price, we'll arrange payment before or after the work is completed. We accept bank transfer and cash. Full payment details will be provided when we confirm your booking.
Are you insured?
+
Yes — we are fully insured. This means you have complete peace of mind on every job we carry out. Our insurance covers both cleaning and waste removal services across all our service areas.
Can I get a quote without committing?
+
Absolutely. All our quotes are completely free and come with no obligation whatsoever. Simply fill in the form above, call us, or send us a WhatsApp — we'll give you an honest, fair quote and you can decide from there.
Do you offer one-off and regular services?
+
Yes — we offer both one-off and regular/recurring services. For commercial cleaning especially, we can set up a regular schedule that works around your business hours. Get in touch to discuss what works best for you.
How do you dispose of waste responsibly?
+
We sort all collected waste and dispose of it in accordance with UK waste regulations. We recycle wherever possible and take all waste to licensed disposal facilities. We are committed to minimising our environmental impact on every job.
