Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner for Cleaning & Waste Removal

Ed Clear & Clean Services was founded with a simple mission: to deliver spotless, reliable, and affordable cleaning and waste removal services to homes and businesses across West Yorkshire.

Based in Huddersfield and serving Leeds, we are a dedicated team committed to making your space cleaner, safer, and more comfortable. Whether you need a thorough end-of-tenancy clean, a post-construction tidy-up, or a full property clearance — we bring professionalism and attention to detail to every single job.

We believe everyone deserves a clean space without the hassle. That's why we offer flexible scheduling, free quotes, and a satisfaction guarantee on all our services.

"We started Ed Clear & Clean because we saw a need for honest, hardworking, and reliable cleaning and clearance services. Our goal is simple — leave every space better than we found it."

— Ed, Founder
